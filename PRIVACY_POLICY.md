# PRIVACY POLICY

**Stardate Revision: 2026.06.03**

Welcome to the **Celestial Observatory** (NASA Daily application, `com.aistudio.nasadaily.jklmn`). Your privacy across the cosmos is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy documents the secure protocols, data preservation principles, and localized telemetry pipelines implemented within this application.

---

## 1. Local-Telemetry First (No Remote Tracking)
The Celestial Observatory operates on a strict **local-first** paradigm. We do not integrate, compile, package, or transmit your individual usage metrics, app navigation loops, hardware identifiers, or screen telemetries to third-party advertising brokers or external analysis servers. 

## 2. API Coupling & External Integrations
Our application fetches real-time astronomy visual logs and astronomical data using public, open-source APIs provided directly by the **National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA)**. 
- While interacting with this celestial feed, the application sends network requests straight to official NASA endpoints.
- No user-identifiable, biographical, or device-specific coordinates are submitted during this synchronization.
- NASA's server logs may record standard network telemetry (e.g., your IP address) subject to their own federal privacy rules; we recommend referencing NASA's official privacy policies for details.

## 3. Local Room SQLite Database Guard
All of your interactive metrics—including bookmarked celestial archives, saved stardates, custom coordinates, and favorite space flight logs:
- Are written directly to a highly secure, offline **Room SQLite Database** residing entirely on your physical device storage.
- There are no cloud synchronized vaults, passive remote mirrors, or secondary offsite server backups created or maintained by this software.
- Uninstalling the application will completely and permanently purge your local offline telemetry vault.

## 4. Native Download Transmissions
If you decide to save high-resolution astronomical visual artifacts locally inside your device's photo gallery, the application broadcasts an intent directly to the standard Android native **Download Manager** service.
- We do not inject custom metadata, hidden tracking tokens, or personal identifiers into downstream image artifacts or saved files.
- The transmission operates strictly on clean, unmonitored standard system channels.
